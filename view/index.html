ABOUT US

Dedicated to providing excellence in patient care, clinical education, and optical services, Eye Q Optometry is ready to serve you. Located in Portland, our doctors are leaders in clinical eye care involving eye and vision disorders. We provide the highest quality lenses, frames and contact lenses to solve the vision needs of our patients. Eye Q Optometry’s customers are consistently happy and satisfied with our polite service and timely fashion.
